what this is

a daily adhd tracker that runs in your browser. no charger required. log mood, focus, energy, wins, impulses, and a brain dump whenever your head's too full. it saves as you type. nothing to submit, nothing to get wrong.

everything stays on this device. no account, no cloud, nobody watching. that's the whole point: it's private. the tradeoff is that your history lives only here, in this browser.

so backups matter. if you clear your browser, switch phones, or something breaks, an export file is the only way to get your data back. use export backup below to download everything as one file, then stash it somewhere safe (a cloud drive, or email it to yourself). tap import backup to bring it all back. do it every week or two and you'll never lose a thing.

the 1 to 5 scales are check-ins, not grades. no perfect score, no streak to protect. a blank day is just a blank day, not a fail. start with today.
